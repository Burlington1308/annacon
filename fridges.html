Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my air conditioner leaking water?

Your aircon is leaking water most likely due to a clogged condensate drain line, dirty air filters causing frozen coils, a damaged drain pan, or improper installation/leveling, leading to water overflow or inability to drain properly. While you can often clean filters and check for major blockages, persistent issues like low refrigerant or component damage require a professional technician to prevent mold and water damage.

Why is my air conditioner not cooling?

Some issues that may result in your air con not cooling are:


  • It may need regassing
  • The compressor may not be running
  • Dirty filters may be restricting air flow
  • You may have a faulty PC board affecting the units functionality

Why is my fridge not cooling?

Check power, clean condenser coils (dusty coils reduce efficiency), ensure door seals are tight, adjust thermostat (ideal fridge: 3-5°C, freezer: -18°C), and unblock air vents.

Why is my fridge leaking water?

Check door seals, clear blocked drain holes, or look for cracked drip pans.
