Is the fridge struggling to start and producing clicking noises? Let us replace your relays and have it running like dream.

Thermostat

A faulty thermostat can result in your fridge humming nonstop and can eventually lead to compressor damage.

PC Board

A faulty pc board on a smart fridge can cause a myriad of problems. From no cooling right down to the fridge not switching on.

Evaporators

Smart fridge evaporators not working will result in the fridge not cooling.

Sensors

Bad sensors on a smart fridge can lead to the fridge not cooling, humming nonstop, overfreezing etc.

Overfreezing

Can be the result of a faulty temperature sensor or faulty heaters.

Leaking

Usually a result of drainage issues or bad door seals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of appliances do you service?

We service a wide range of appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, tumble dryers, and air conditioning units.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide emergency repair services for urgent issues. Please contact us directly for immediate assistance.

Why is my fridge not cooling?

Check power, clean condenser coils (dusty coils reduce efficiency), ensure door seals are tight, adjust thermostat (ideal fridge: 3-5°C, freezer: -18°C), and unblock air vents.

Why is my fridge leaking water?

Check door seals, clear blocked drain holes, or look for cracked drip pans.

Excessive ice build up

Often a defrost system issue or poor door seal letting in warm air.
