Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide emergency repair services for urgent issues. Please contact us directly for immediate assistance.

How often should a cold room be serviced?

Your cold room or wal-in freezer should be serviced every 3-6 months. Inspect things like door seals, clean the condesor/evaporator coils, check fan motors and monitor temperature/humidity.

What are the signs that my cold room needs repair?

Common indicators include inconsistent internal temperatures, unusual noises (buzzing or grinding), frost or ice buildup on walls or coils, condensation on the inside of the room, and food spoiling before its expiration date.

Why is my cold room not reaching the set temperature?

Potential causes include damaged door seals (gaskets), low refrigerant levels, dirty condenser or evaporator coils, faulty compressors, or blocked air vents.

What does excessive ice buildup mean?

This typically indicates poor airflow from overloading the room, a faulty defrost cycle, or moisture entering through a damaged door seal.

What causes unusual noises?

A buzzing sound often points to a dirty cooling fan, while a rattling or loud humming can indicate a failing compressor or loose motor components.
