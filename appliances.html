Frequently Asked Questions

What types of appliances do you service?

We service a wide range of appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, tumble dryers, and air conditioning units.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide emergency repair services for urgent issues. Please contact us directly for immediate assistance.

Why is my washing machine not draining/spinning?

Check the drain hose for kinks, clear the filter/pump, ensure the lid is shut, and check the drive belt for wear.

Why is my washin machine leaking?

Inspect hoses for cracks/loose connections, check the detergent drawer for overflow (too much soap!), or look for pump seal issues.

My washing machine won't start

Confirm it's plugged in, the door is latched, the breaker isn't tripped, and reset the machine (unplug for a minute). If none of this helps then call us to take a look.

Why is my dryer not drying my clothes>

Check for overloaded machine, clogged vents/hoses, tripped circuit breaker, or a faulty heating element/thermal fuse.

Why is my dryer not spinning?

A broken or loose drive belt is usually the cause, though a bad motor or timer could also be responsible.

My dryer is blowing cold air

Test the thermal fuse, heating element, and igniter (gas) or thermostat (electric) for continuity with a multimeter.
